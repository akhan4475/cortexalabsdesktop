Meet Our
Leadership Team

Leading the way through innovation

From humble beginnings in real estate and home flipping, our owner discovered a passion for construction and saw firsthand the importance of reliable work in every phase of a home’s transformation. After years of perfecting property renovations, he realized the vast potential of focusing on one critical element—strong and dependable roofing. This spark led to the launch of Silver Ridge Roofing in 2023.

Driven by the philosophy that customers deserve top quality and transparent service, he set out to create a young and dynamic roofing brand. Today, his journey continues as the head of a rapidly growing team. By combining innovative thinking, unwavering honesty, and genuine concern for the community, he has transformed a simple vision into a business that is redefining roofing services in Texas and beyond.

The Team Behind
the Vision

Our team is built on a shared commitment to doing right by every homeowner. We come from diverse backgrounds but unite under one goal: delivering top-notch service guided by transparency, care, and a passion for getting the job done right. From in-office support to on-site professionals, each member of our team brings a unique set of skills and a willingness to go the extra mile. Together, we strive to create a roofing experience that homeowners trust, appreciate, and recommend to friends and neighbors.

Your
Roofing
Questions
Answered

What financing options do you provide?

We partner with reputable lenders to offer flexible payment plans, including zero-interest options for up to 36 months. Our goal is to complete your roofing project without straining your budget.

Do you handle both residential and commercial projects?

Absolutely. While we specialize in residential roofing, our expertise also extends to a variety of commercial systems. Our certified team applies the same high standards of safety, durability, and workmanship to every project we undertake.

Do you help with insurance claims?

Absolutely. We collaborate with your insurance company and advocate for the coverage you deserve. Our goal is to make the claims process stress-free and transparent from start to finish.

Do you offer free inspections?

Yes. We provide a no-cost, comprehensive inspection to thoroughly assess your roof and recommend tailored solutions based on its unique needs.

Are you certified or recognized by any organizations?

Absolutely. We hold certifications from leading manufacturers like GAF, CertainTeed, and Malarkey. We’re also BBB accredited, reflecting our commitment to quality and customer trust.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes. We provide manufacturer-backed warranties, with options for limited lifetime coverage depending on your chosen materials. This ensures you receive long-term protection and peace of mind.
