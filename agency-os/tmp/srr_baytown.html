Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my roof inspected in Houston?

We recommend a professional roof inspection at least once a year and after major storms. Regular inspections help catch small issues before they turn into costly repairs.

What type of roofing material is best for Houston’s climate?

Asphalt shingles, metal roofing, and tile roofs are great options for Houston. Metal roofing is particularly durable against hurricanes, while cool roofing options help reflect heat and lower energy costs.

How long does a typical roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements take 1 to 3 days, depending on the size and complexity of the roof. Weather conditions and material availability can also impact the timeline.

Does homeowners insurance cover roof damage in Houston?

Insurance typically covers storm and hail damage, but coverage varies by policy. We assist with insurance claims to help homeowners get the compensation they deserve.

What are the signs that I need a roof replacement instead of repairs in Houston?

If your roof is over 20 years old and has widespread damage, frequent leaks, or missing shingles, it may be time for a replacement. Our team provides honest assessments to help you decide.
