## Core Philosophy

Permission + video + relentless follow-up = appointments.

The DM Sorcery system (Charlie Morgan, $5.8M from cold DMs) is the primary framework. Every step asks for permission before advancing. The video does the closing — follow-ups only exist to get them to watch the video. Once they gave permission, you have permission to follow up relentlessly.

**Contractors have a high BS detector.** They've been pitched by every marketing agency and SEO scammer alive. The permission-based approach disarms them because no one else is polite enough to ask first.

**Know their business before you contact them.** First name only = spam bot. First name + you know they're a pool contractor in Phoenix with no mobile site = someone who did their homework. (Otto Frederick lesson — this is what breaks most DM systems in execution.)

## Full Sequence — DM Sorcery Adapted for Instagram + Contractors

### Step 0 — Research Before Contacting (5 min per lead)
Before sending anything:
- [ ] Look up their business on Google Maps (niche, city, review count, rating)
- [ ] Open their website (if exists) — note 1-2 specific issues
- [ ] Note if they have no site at all
- [ ] Find their Instagram handle

### Step 1 — Connect (Follow on Instagram)
Follow their Instagram account. Wait for them to follow back OR send the Trojan Horse anyway after 24h.

### Step 2 — Trojan Horse (Voice Note on IG — NOT text)
Send a voice note or short video, NOT a written message. Voice/video breaks the pattern — every other pitcher sends text.

```
"Hey [first name], thanks for following back.
I looked up [business name] and I work with [pool/roofing/landscaping] contractors specifically.
I've got something I think could actually help your business, but I don't want to pitch anything without your permission.
If you're open to hearing it, just reply with a thumbs up."
```

Key: "I looked up [business name]" — shows you actually know who they are. This one sentence separates you from every template-blaster they've ever seen.

CTA: **Thumbs up only.** One word. Dead easy to send. They don't have to think.

### Step 3 — If No Reply: 9 Follow-Ups on Trojan Horse
Each follow-up exists ONLY to get them to click and listen to the voice note. Not to pitch. Not to sell.

Follow-ups can be:
- GIFs (waiting GIF, "hello is anyone there" meme, Pablo Escobar swing meme)
- Casual bumps: "Hey [name] — just checking if the voice note actually came through 👀"
- Pattern interrupts: "Oi [name] — DMing before you get too famous 😅"
- Light self-deprecating: "Last message I promise. Clearly bad at this part of my job."

80% of responses come at follow-up 7–9. Most people quit at 3. Don't quit.

Note for contractors: don't over-rely on memes. Mix in genuine curiosity bumps. Experienced business owners get annoyed by pure meme bombing. Keep 50% casual text, 50% memes/GIFs.

### Step 4 — They Reply (Thumbs Up) → Send Native VSL
**Critical: Native video inside Instagram DMs. 1:59 max. iPhone selfie. Do NOT send a Loom link.**

Sending external links (Loom, YouTube) loses them. They stay in DMs, video plays in DMs. That's the system.

Record once. Reuse it with slight intro personalization if needed.

**VSL Script Structure:**
```
[0:00–0:10] Transparency + permission maintain
"Hey [name], appreciate the thumbs up. Fair warning — I am here to show you something I built for [niche] contractors, totally fine to click off anytime."

[0:10–0:20] Offer (1 sentence, outcome-focused)
"I build websites specifically for [pool/roofing/landscaping] contractors in [city/region] that actually bring you inbound jobs — not just a site that sits there."

[0:20–0:50] Show their current situation briefly (screen share their site or Google listing)
"So I pulled up [business name]'s site — [1 specific observation]. [1 more observation if relevant]."

[0:50–1:20] Proof (testimonial clip OR well-built example if no clients yet)
"Here's what a site built to convert looks like for a [niche] contractor..."
[Show example site or describe the gap clearly]

[1:20–1:45] Method (2 sentences max — no fluff)
"It takes about [7-14] days and I handle everything. You don't need to do anything except give me content and feedback."

[1:45–1:59] CTA — same as Trojan Horse
"If you want to see what this could look like for [business name] specifically, just reply with a thumbs up and I'll send over a booking link for a quick 20-minute call."
```

### Step 5 — If No Reply After VSL: 9 More Follow-Ups
Same energy as step 3 — getting them to re-watch the video.

```
Day 1 after VSL: "Hey [name] — not sure if the video came through. Did it play?"
Day 3: GIF of someone waiting
Day 5: "Quick question — did any of that resonate with what you're seeing for [business name]?"
Day 7: "Still here if you want to chat [name]"
Day 9: "Last one from me — [thumbs up emoji] if you want the cal link and I'll leave you alone"
(Continue through 9 total)
```

### Step 6 — They Say Thumbs Up Again → Send Calendar Link Natively
Don't send a naked Cal.com URL. Write it in context:

```
"Perfect — here's a link to grab 20 minutes: [cal.com link]
Pick any slot that works and I'll walk you through exactly what I'd build for [business name]."
```

### Step 7 — Multi-Channel Expansion
If they're not responding on Instagram but showed some signal:
- Find their Facebook — send same Trojan Horse there
- Find their email — send short permission email
- Cross-reference: "Hey [name] — also sent this on Instagram in case this is easier"

Never stay on one channel alone.

## Banned Phrases

Never use — kills credibility or breaks the system:
- "I wanted to reach out because..."
- "I help businesses like yours..."
- "Are you open to..."
- "We specialize in..."
- "Book a free call"
- "Limited spots available"
- "I'm not selling anything" (you are — own it)
- Sending a Loom link as the first video (external = lose them)
- Paragraph-long first message
- Three or more sentences before they've replied once
- Emojis in the Trojan Horse voice note
- Never correct a prospect who's factually wrong — find a path forward instead

## Reply Frameworks

### They replied positively ("interested", "what do you do", "tell me more")
Move to VSL immediately. Don't pitch over text.
```
"Nice — sending you a quick video now. 2 minutes, worth it I promise."
[Send native VSL]
```

### They replied skeptically ("what is this for" / "who are you")
```
"Fair question — I build websites for [niche] contractors in [city]. Noticed [business name]'s [site/listing] and had a thought.
Not trying to waste your time — if it's not relevant just say so. But if you give me 2 minutes I think it'll be worth it."
```

### They replied with "I've tried this before / not interested"
Apply Alicia Joseph method — get curious:
```
"That's interesting — what didn't work about it?"
```
Then listen. Understand the real objection. Respond to that specifically.

### They pushed back / got defensive
Apply Otto Frederick lesson — never match their energy:
```
"Totally fair — I appreciate you being straight with me. What would make it easier to look at this?"
```
Stay positive. Never defend. Find an opening.

### They said "contact me on [different platform]"
Go there. Immediately. Create the account if needed. Takes 3 minutes. Shows conviction.

### They asked for price before call
```
"Depends on the scope — [niche] contractor sites run between $600 and $1,100 depending on what you need. 
Easier to give you the right number after 20 minutes. Worth a quick call?"
```

## Pre-Contact Research Checklist

For every lead before first contact:
- [ ] Business name confirmed (not a franchise/national chain)
- [ ] Niche confirmed (pool/roofing/landscaping/remodeling/construction/painters)
- [ ] City confirmed
- [ ] Review count ≥ 3 (ICP minimum)
- [ ] Website URL noted (or confirmed they have none)
- [ ] 1-2 specific site observations ready (for VSL and Trojan Horse)
- [ ] Instagram handle confirmed active

---

## Instagram DM Volume Guidelines

Daily limits:
- New account (< 6 months): 20–50 DMs/day
- Older account (6+ months): 100–150 DMs/day

Start at 30/day, increase by 10 per week. Don't rush the ceiling — account restriction kills momentum.

---

## Quality Checklist

Before sending any DM:
- [ ] Did pre-contact research (know their niche, city, site status)
- [ ] Trojan Horse is voice note or video — NOT text
- [ ] CTA is one word (thumbs up / ok / yes)
- [ ] VSL is under 1:59 and recorded as native IG DM video
- [ ] No external links in VSL — native only
- [ ] Follow-ups are varied — not same message repeated
- [ ] Logged in Supabase with `dm_sent_at` timestamp
- [ ] Follow-up schedule set (days 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 11, 14, 17, 21)
