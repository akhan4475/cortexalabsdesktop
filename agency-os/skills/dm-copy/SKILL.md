## Banned Phrases

Never use these — they kill credibility immediately:

- "I help businesses like yours..."
- "Are you open to..."
- "I wanted to reach out because..."
- "We specialize in..."
- "As a business owner..."
- "I noticed your website could use some work"
- Any mention of AI, automation, or Claude
- "Limited spots available"
- "Book a free call"
- Emojis in first message to cold prospect
- Three or more sentences in opening DM

---

## Quality Checklist

Before sending any DM, verify:

- [ ] Opens with something specific to THEM (not generic)
- [ ] No "I help" or banned phrase in first line
- [ ] Under 5 sentences total
- [ ] No pitch in first message — only curiosity/question
- [ ] Written as Ayaan personally (not agency voice)
- [ ] No mention of price, packages, or AI
- [ ] CTA is a question, not a request to book
- [ ] Passes the "would a real person send this?" test
