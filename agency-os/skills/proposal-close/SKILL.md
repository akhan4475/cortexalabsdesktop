How to write, send, and follow up on proposals. What goes in the proposal, how to present it, and the exact close sequence once it's sent.

## When to Send a Proposal

Send after:
- Discovery call held (`call_held` stage)
- Clear pain identified (they have a problem online presence can solve)
- Budget range confirmed (at least implied they can afford it)
- They agreed to review a proposal

Do NOT send a proposal to:
- Leads who haven't been on a call
- Leads who said "just send me some info" without a call
- Leads where budget is clearly misaligned
