## What This Skill Covers

How to record and send personalized Loom videos to warm prospects (leads that replied or are in `replied` stage). Looms are recorded as Ayaan personally — never mention AI or automation.

---

## When to Send a Loom

Send a Loom when a lead:
- Replies with interest or curiosity (even vague "tell me more")
- Has a visible website you can screen-record
- Is at `replied` stage and needs more than text to convert to a call

Do NOT send a Loom to:
- Cold prospects who haven't replied
- Leads already at `call_booked` or beyond
- Leads with no online presence to show on screen

## Personalization Data Points to Gather Before Recording

Pull these before hitting record:

- [ ] Google Maps listing (rating, reviews, photos)
- [ ] Their website (if exists) — screenshot the homepage
- [ ] Competitor websites in their city
- [ ] One specific thing about their business (specialty, service area, years in business)
- [ ] Any recent reviews (positive ones to mention)

---

## Screen Recording Checklist

Before hitting record:

- [ ] Close all unnecessary tabs
- [ ] Clean browser bookmarks bar hidden
- [ ] Loom Chrome extension ready
- [ ] Start on THEIR Google listing or website
- [ ] Face cam enabled (shows authenticity)
- [ ] Quiet environment
- [ ] Under 3 minutes total
