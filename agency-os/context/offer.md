# CortexaLabs AI — Offer & Pricing

## Core Value Proposition

Contractors with bad or no websites are losing $500–$5,000/month in missed leads. We fix that with a professional, mobile-first, conversion-optimized site built specifically for their trade — delivered in 7–14 days for a one-time fee.

The pitch is not "here is a pretty website." The pitch is: "Your current online presence is costing you money every month. We stop the bleeding for a one-time investment."

## Offer Framing on Calls

**The math close:**
"What's your average job value? [$X]. If your site generates even one extra call a month that converts — that's $X in revenue. Our site pays for itself in [days/weeks]. After that it's pure profit."

**The competitor close:**
"I pulled up your top competitor in [city] while I was prepping for this call. Their site has [X, Y, Z]. Right now, when someone Googles '[niche] in [city],' the business with the better-looking site gets the call. That's losing you money today."

**The risk reversal (Authority tier):**
"We guarantee at least 5 contact form submissions in 90 days or we redesign the conversion elements for free. We only do that because we're confident it works."

## Tier Recommendation Logic

Default recommendation: **Pro** ($800). It covers 80% of what most contractors need and the price is easy to justify.

Upgrade to Authority if:
- Contractor is established (5+ years, 20+ reviews)
- They mention wanting more bookings (not just calls)
- They have a good reputation but weak web presence
- They are in a competitive market (major metro)

Stick with Starter if:
- Contractor is brand new (under 2 years, under 10 reviews)
- Budget sensitivity is clear
- They just need something professional to exist
