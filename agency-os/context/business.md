# CortexaLabs AI — Business Overview

## What We Do

CortexaLabs AI builds high-converting websites for local contractors. We are not a general web agency — we specialize exclusively in the trades: pool contractors, roofers, landscapers, remodelers, general contractors, and painters. Every site we build is engineered to convert a visitor searching Google into a booked estimate.

We are a done-for-you service. The contractor pays once. We deliver a professional, mobile-optimized site in 7–14 days. They get more calls, more leads, more jobs.

## Revenue Model

This is a one-time fee service. No recurring retainer for web design.

- **Primary revenue:** Site builds ($600–$1,100 per project)
- **Secondary revenue (upsells):**
  - Hosting & maintenance: $97/month
  - Local SEO management: $297/month
  - Google Ads management: $497/month
- **Goal Year 1:** 4 clients/month = $3,200–$4,400 MRR in upsells + $2,400–$4,400/month in builds
- **Goal Year 1 total:** $50,000+ revenue

## Why Contractor Websites

Local contractors are among the most underserved business categories online. Most have:
- No website at all, or a site built 8+ years ago
- Sites that don't load on mobile
- No Google Business integration
- No clear CTA or contact form
- No trust signals (reviews, licenses, photos of work)

They also have strong business fundamentals — real jobs, repeat customers, high transaction values ($3,000–$50,000 per project). They are willing to pay for leads. A website that generates 2 extra calls a month at a $5,000 average job pays for itself in the first week.

The pitch is not "get a nice website." The pitch is: "You're losing $500–$5,000/month right now. We fix that for a one-time fee of $600."

## What Makes Us Different

1. **Niche-specific templates:** Our sites are built for contractors, not adapted from generic templates. They have the right sections (services, service areas, reviews, trust signals, booking widget).
2. **Speed:** We deliver in 7–14 days. Most agencies take 4–8 weeks.
3. **One-time fee:** Contractors hate subscriptions. We charge once.
4. **Personalized Loom outreach:** Every prospect sees a 60-second video of us reviewing their specific business before they ever hear our pitch. Close rate is 3–5x cold email.
5. **Contractor-native language:** We do not talk to contractors like they are tech startups. Our copy speaks their language.
