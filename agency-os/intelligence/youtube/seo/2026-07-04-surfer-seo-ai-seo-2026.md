## Key Tactics

1. **SEO is not dead — it's the foundation of AI search** — Google grew 20% last year, sends 190x more traffic to websites than ChatGPT. ChatGPT uses Bing's index; Perplexity uses Bing's index. AI assistants cite pages that already rank in traditional search. If your site doesn't rank in Google, it's invisible in AI search too.
2. **Money pages first, clusters second** — Build all transactional/service pages before any blog content. These are the pages that convert visitors to customers. Blogs without money pages = traffic to nowhere. The pattern: money page → mid-funnel comparison content → top-funnel educational content (in that build order).
3. **Keyword Sweet Spot framework** — Four attributes: demand (search volume), fit (funnel stage), intent (what the searcher wants), difficulty (can you rank?). A keyword must pass all four to be worth targeting. Unicorn keywords score high on all four — prioritize them.
4. **Topical authority beats domain authority on specific clusters** — A DR42 plumbing-only site outranks DR90 general sites for plumbing queries. Cover a topic from every angle (definition, how-to, comparison, deep dive) and Google recognizes you as the authority on that subject. Cluster-first strategy accelerates this.
5. **Build one full cluster before starting the next** — Pick one topic cluster connected to a money page. Build it out completely: money page → comparison posts → educational posts → internal links connecting all of them. Only then start the next cluster. Scattered content across 10 topics = slow results. Deep coverage of 1 topic = topical authority and faster ranking.
6. **AI-generated content needs the human layer to rank** — AI can handle structure, scaffolding, and sentence flow. You add: personal experience, opinions, original data (your results/case studies), expert insights, and specific real examples. Pages with no human layer = AI slop = zero traffic (96% of all web pages get zero Google traffic per Ahrefs study of 14 billion pages).
7. **Surface-level content doesn't earn clicks anymore** — AI overviews now summarize informational, commercial, AND transactional queries. Your content must go deeper than the AI overview to earn the click. Zero-click is rising — only comprehensive, expert content earns traffic.
8. **30-day action plan works** — Week 1: set up GSC, keyword research, identify money pages. Week 2: write/optimize main money page with full on-page checklist. Week 3: publish first cluster post, internal link to money page. Week 4: journalist queries (Source of Sources, 15 min/day), check AI visibility, plan next cluster.
9. **Best link building for beginners: journalist queries** — Sign up for Source of Sources (free). Respond to journalist queries with expert insights. When published, you get a backlink. Numbers game (100 submits → 1 link), but takes only 15 min/day and requires zero cold outreach to webmasters.
10. **Linkable assets are backlink magnets** — Free tools, calculators, templates, comprehensive guides. Claude Code makes building simple browser tools accessible to non-technical marketers. A coffee-to-water ratio calculator = backlinks from every coffee blog. For contractors: a "roofing cost estimator" calculator would earn links from every home improvement site.
11. **AI visibility tracking is now required** — Google Search Console doesn't capture ChatGPT/Perplexity traffic. You need to manually check (or use Surfer's AI tracker) whether your brand appears when AI assistants answer questions in your space. Competitors who track this adapt; those who don't get outpaced.
12. **Screaming Frog for technical SEO audits** — Free for up to 500 URLs. Crawls your entire site, surfaces broken links, missing title tags, duplicate content, redirect chains. Run it before launching any new site. Fixes prioritized issues faster than manual review.
13. **Local businesses: Google Business Profile is the highest-leverage SEO action** — Claim, fill out every field, get reviews, respond to every review. This alone puts you in map results for "[niche] near me" searches. More important than any blog post for local service businesses in the first 90 days.
14. **Content research before writing** — Open the top 5 results for your target keyword. Read all of them. Find: (a) topics they all cover = table stakes you must include, (b) what none cover = your differentiation opportunity, (c) average length benchmark, (d) unique angles taken. Do this manually for first 5 pieces to understand what you're automating.
15. **On-page SEO checklist must-hits** — Title tag: keyword + under 60 chars + compelling. H1: mirrors title tag. H2s: main sections with keywords naturally. URL: short, descriptive, keyword-included. Meta description: 150–160 chars, action-oriented, keyword included. Internal links: connect every piece to related pages and money pages. Images: descriptive filenames + alt text.

## Frameworks

### Keyword Sweet Spot — 4-Point Filter
```
1. DEMAND: Does it get searched? (Check search volume)
2. FIT: Where is it in the funnel? (Top/mid/bottom — know which)
3. INTENT: What does the searcher want? (Google top 10 tells you)
4. DIFFICULTY: Can you realistically rank? (Site authority vs. competition)

Prioritize keywords that score well on all four.
For new sites: start with long-tail, low-difficulty keywords.
Build authority on those, then work up to competitive head terms.
```

### Funnel Build Order (Bottom-Up)
```
STEP 1 — Money Pages (bottom of funnel)
  → Transactional keywords: "[service] [city]", "hire [niche] contractor"
  → These pages pay the bills — build them first, period

STEP 2 — One Cluster at a Time (mid + top funnel)
  → Pick cluster connected to one money page
  → Build: comparison content (commercial intent) → educational content (informational)
  → Every piece internally links back to the money page

STEP 3 — Repeat by Cluster
  → Complete one cluster fully before starting next
  → Topical authority compounds: more coverage of one topic → easier to rank for all keywords in it

DON'T: scatter content across 10 random topics in parallel
DO: go deep on one topic → establish authority → move to next
```

### AI Search Optimization (Same as Good SEO)
```
To show up in ChatGPT/Perplexity answers:
  1. Comprehensive content with clear subheadings + direct answers
  2. Topical authority through clusters (AI picks authorities, not pages)
  3. Earn citations from authoritative sites (backlinks = AI citation signals)
  4. Get into "best-of" roundup articles in your niche
  5. Track brand mentions in AI queries (manual or Surfer AI tracker)

Key insight: AI search optimization = doing good SEO well.
No separate discipline needed. Good rankings → AI visibility.
```

### On-Page Checklist (Every Page)
```
TITLE: keyword + under 60 chars + benefit/hook
H1: mirrors or close variant of title tag
H2s: main sections, keyword variations naturally placed
URL: /short-descriptive-keyword-slug
META DESC: 150-160 chars, action-oriented, includes keyword, ad-copy mindset

INTERNAL LINKS:
  → From this page to: 2+ related posts, 1+ money page
  → From related posts to: this page (retroactively)

IMAGES:
  → Descriptive filename (not IMG_001.jpg)
  → Alt text describes the image (for screen readers + Google)
  → WebP format, compressed

SCHEMA: Article or LocalBusiness markup where applicable
```
