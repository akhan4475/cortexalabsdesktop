## Key Tactics

1. **WAS = Website as a Service.** One build fee (his model: $1,000, split $500 up front / $500 on delivery) + recurring monthly fee (his model: $99/mo) that bundles hosting, maintenance, and a lead system — not just "a website," a maintained asset.
2. **Full deliverable checklist for the monthly fee** (his model): secure hosting, monthly edits (phone/email/content changes), lead capture system (forms + call buttons), basic SEO (keywords, page titles), Google Business Profile optimization, automated lead follow-up (system contacts the lead, then notifies the client). Framed explicitly as "replaces a full-time assistant" — that's the retention pitch.
3. **Website page structure (conversion-focused spec):** Hero (headline + CTA visible within 3 seconds) → Services → Why Choose Us (trust: licensed/insured/reviews) → Photo gallery of real work → About → Service area map → Form, positioned so it's reachable no matter where the visitor scrolls.
4. **Upsell ladder (his pricing, for reference):** Local SEO $300-700/mo, Meta ad management $600-1,500/mo, advanced automations $50-150/mo. Claim: a single $99/mo client can be walked up to $3,000+/mo total by upselling the same client rather than closing new ones.
5. **3 prospect types to search for on Google Maps:**
   - No website link at all on the Google Business Profile — easiest close
   - Old/broken site — not mobile-friendly, zero CTAs, visibly outdated
   - Great reviews but a bad website — reputation exists in person, digital presence doesn't match it
6. **Preview-first outreach method:** build the prospect's site before ever asking for money (swap business name/niche/photos/services into a template, ~5-10 min), record a Loom walking through it, then send the Loom cold — the video does the selling, not the pitch.
7. **48h follow-up stat cited by creator:** most people who send a message and don't get a reply give up immediately; his claim is most of his own closes happen on the 2nd or 3rd follow-up, not the first message.

## Frameworks

### WAS Deliverable Stack (what the monthly fee buys, per creator's model)
```
Build (one-time):     Professional mobile-optimized conversion-focused website
Monthly fee includes: Hosting + monthly edits + lead capture (forms/call buttons)
                       + basic SEO (keywords/titles) + GBP optimization
                       + automated lead follow-up
```

### Website Page Spec
```
1. Hero — headline + service + CTA button, must land within 3 seconds
2. Services
3. Why Choose Us — licensed/insured/reviews (trust signals)
4. Photo gallery — real client work
5. About
6. Service area map
7. Form — persistent regardless of scroll position
```

### Upsell Ladder (reference pricing, not CortexaLabs' actual pricing)
```
Base:          $99/mo hosting
+ Local SEO:   $300-700/mo
+ Meta ads:    $600-1,500/mo
+ Automations: $50-150/mo
= up to $3,000+/mo from one client via upsells alone
```

### 3 Prospect Types (Google Maps search)
```
Type 1: No website link on GBP at all         → easiest close
Type 2: Old/broken site, not mobile, no CTA   → visible, obvious gap
Type 3: Great reviews, bad website            → reputation/digital mismatch
```
