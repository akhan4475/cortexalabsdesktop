## Key Tactics

1. **Profile funnel over direct-response ads** — Drive paid traffic to an IG profile, not a landing page. The profile does the trust-building; the setter does the conversion. Direct VSL ads are oversaturated and yield 1-2x ROAS vs 12x with this funnel.
2. **80/20 rule: marketing converts, sales closes** — Let content (posts + story sequences) do 80% of trust/conversion work so the setter/closer only needs to do 20%. Keeps team lean (3 people closing $151k/mo).
3. **Daily story sequences as primary trust mechanism** — Post a story sequence every single day following this order: (1) retention hook, (2) booking/meeting proof, (3) client result stack (multiple), (4) FOMO pain point, (5) lead magnet CTA. This creates "I need to buy from this person" over time.
4. **Setter DMs all engaged followers and profile visitors** — Human setter reaches out to everyone who follows or engages. At scale: 1,500 meetings booked over 365 days, $700 cash collected per meeting taken (before close).
5. **Track cash per ad, not cost per lead** — Optimize for ROAS (cash collected per ad dollar), not CPL or CPB. A $200 CPB ad can outperform a $80 CPB ad if close rate is higher.
6. **Three ad creative types**: Action-driven (CTA to follow), Problem-solution (pain → solution → follow), Credibility-driven (authority + results shown = follow). Test 100+ hooks — most agencies test 3 and quit.
7. **Content funnel needs all 3 layers simultaneously** — Top (vlogs, awareness), Middle (client interviews, nurturing), Bottom (niche-specific how-to, conversion). Missing any layer kills the funnel.
8. **No guarantee needed with enough content trust** — When marketing does enough trust-building, removing the guarantee filters for serious buyers and increases deal quality. "Trust is the new currency in 2025."
9. **Dynamic asset over VSL** — Replace traditional VSL with a credibility reel/asset that pre-builds trust so prospects show up to calls already sold. 80% show-up rate achieved.
10. **Launch sprint: 300 inbound DMs in 24 hours** — Run a concentrated story sequence sprint + lead magnet → collected $101k in under 3 weeks from resulting inbound.

## Frameworks

### The Profile Funnel (Full Stack)
```
Paid Ads (video / carousel / story boost)
    ↓
Instagram Profile (trust hub)
    ↓
Daily Content System
  ├── Short-form posts (daily)
  ├── Story sequences (daily — 5-part sequence above)
  └── Long-form YouTube (awareness layer)
    ↓
Setter (DMs all followers + engagers)
    ↓
Dynamic Asset (credibility reel, not VSL)
    ↓
Lead Magnet (for unconverted warm leads)
    ↓
Sales Call → Close
    ↓
Follow-up Sequence (for no-shows / no-closes → back to top)
```

### Story Sequence Formula (5-Part Daily)
1. **Retention hook** — Something visually attention-grabbing (e.g. two people, contrast visual)
2. **Meeting/booking proof** — "Last 26 days: 122 calls booked, 98 showed up"
3. **Client result stack** — 3-5 client wins back to back, no commentary
4. **FOMO pain point** — "While you're struggling signing 1-2K clients, my people pay me $5-10K in the DMs"
5. **Lead magnet / CTA** — Offer something free to DM for, or book a call

### Content Layer System
- **Top of funnel** (awareness): Vlogs, full courses, storytelling, broad topics
- **Middle** (nurturing): Client interviews, breakdowns, case study walkthroughs
- **Bottom** (conversion): Niche-specific tactical content, exact-problem-exact-solution

### Unit Economics Tracking Model
- Track: ad spend → profile visits → followers → setter conversations → qualified appointments → show-ups → cash collected
- Key metrics: Cost per qualified booking ($83 in Nik's market), show-up rate (80%), cash per meeting taken ($700), cash per close ($13,550)
- Never optimize for CPL alone — always trace to cash

## Raw Notes

Nik Setting runs a consulting business at $3M+/year profit. This video breaks down the exact funnel he uses to acquire clients via Instagram without relying on traditional VSL funnels.

Core premise: the market in 2025 has seen too many promises and guarantees. Trust is now the differentiator. The profile funnel builds trust at scale through content so the setter only needs to handle logistics (booking), not selling.

Numbers from January 2026:
- Ad spend: $12,825
- 2,965 profile visits at $0.23/visit
- 14% follower conversion rate → ~415 followers
- $5/qualified follower
- Setter reached out to all → 148 qualified conversations
- 146 appointments booked → $83/qualified booking
- 101 show-ups (80% show rate) → $120/qualified show-up
- Cash collected: $151,000 (12.4x ROAS on this funnel alone)
- Separate entry-to-asset ads: $1,300 spend → $9,000 collected (8.78x ROAS)

Content system: daily posts + daily story sequences + YouTube long-form. Content manager handles all of this. Setter is a separate hire who DMs everyone.

Nik does not boost posts — uses ads manager properly. Boosting "stopped working two years ago." Uses three ad types (action-driven, problem-solution, credibility-driven) and has tested 100+ hooks.

Retargeting: top-of-funnel ad viewers get retargeted back into the content ecosystem constantly.

Dynamic asset = not a VSL. It's a credibility reel that does trust-building before the sales call so the closer has an easy job.

For no-shows / non-closes: follow-up sequence pushes them back into the top of funnel content loop.
