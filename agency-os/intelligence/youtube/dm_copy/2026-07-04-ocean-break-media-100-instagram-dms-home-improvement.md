## Key Tactics

1. **Volume over personalization at DM stage** — Same exact DM copy sent to all 100 leads. Only variable swapped was the prospect's local area (town/city). Personalized copy not required to get initial response.
2. **Scrape Google Maps → find IG → DM** — Use Phantom Buster (free trial via temp email) to scrape Google Maps leads by niche + city. Then manually find each business's Instagram, copy-paste the DM.
3. **Instagram DM daily limits** — Newer accounts (< 6 months): 20–50 DMs/day. Older accounts (6+ months): 100–150 DMs/day. Do not exceed limits or risk account restriction.
4. **Lead-focused opener (value frame)** — "Would you be interested in [X] leads for your [niche] business in [area]?" — Hooks with outcome (leads), not with services. Bypasses the "what do you sell?" skepticism.
5. **Exclude franchise/national brands** — When scraping, skip large recognizable names (Home Depot, Lowe's etc.). Target local owner-operated businesses only.
6. **Google Maps as primary lead source for local niches** — Reliable, filterable by niche + city, most local businesses have a listing. Use it before Instagram-native search.

## Frameworks

### Volume DM Workflow
```
Phantom Buster → Google Maps scrape (niche + city)
    ↓
Export lead list (business name + address + phone)
    ↓
Manually find each business's Instagram profile
    ↓
Copy-paste DM (same text, swap [area] variable)
    ↓
Send up to 100/day (old account) or 50/day (new account)
    ↓
Follow up next day: check who replied
    ↓
Engage replies → book calls
```

### DM Template (Value-Frame)
```
"Would you be interested in [outcome] for your [niche] business in [area]?"

Example:
"Would you be interested in 10 leads a month for your home improvement business in Charlotte?"
```

## How to Apply

CortexaLabs can use the same Google Maps → Instagram scraping workflow for pool, roofing, landscaping, remodeling, and construction niches. Adapt the opener to website leads rather than generic "leads": "Would you be interested in a website that brings you [X] inbound jobs per month in [city]?" This keeps the lead-outcome framing while being specific to our offer.

Use Apify (not Phantom Buster) since it's already integrated. The DM template logic applies directly: one opener template, swap niche + city, high volume, replies get moved to Loom follow-up.
