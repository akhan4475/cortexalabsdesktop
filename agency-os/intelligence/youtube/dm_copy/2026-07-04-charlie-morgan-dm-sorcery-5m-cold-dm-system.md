## Key Tactics

1. **Connect before you DM** — Follow/friend request first. You need connection before messaging or they won't see it. On Instagram: follow them. On Facebook: friend request.
2. **Trojan Horse soft pitch (voice note or video, NOT text)** — First message after connecting is a voice note or video saying: "Hey [name], I know you get pitched here all the time. I want to pitch you something but I'd like to ask your permission first. If it's okay, just reply [CTA word]." This puts them in the driver's seat and breaks the pattern because no one else is polite enough to ask.
3. **Permission CTA is ONE word** — "thumbs up", "ok", "yes". Simple. They don't have to think. Consistency bias: once they do it, they're more likely to do it again.
4. **Native VSL (1:59 max)** — Once they grant permission, send a native selfie video shot on iPhone. Never Loom, never professional camera, never a link to an external site. Keep them ON the app. 1:59 is the limit most platforms enforce for native video DMs.
5. **VSL structure: transparency → offer → proof → method → same CTA** — Start: "I'm here to sell you something, you've given me permission, click off anytime." Then offer, then proof (testimonials or Miro diagram if no testimonials), then brief how, then: "If I have your permission, I'd love to send over a calendar link — just reply [same CTA word from step 1]."
6. **Use the SAME CTA word throughout** — Whatever micro-commitment they gave in step 1 (thumbs up), use it again in the VSL. Commitment/consistency bias compounds. They're already familiar with the action.
7. **9 follow-ups on the permission phase** — If they didn't respond to the voice note, follow up 9 times. Not "just following up." Use memes, GIFs, funny messages, pattern interrupts. "Hey, what did you think of the video?" — makes them curious to click and watch. 80% of Calendly links are sent after follow-up 7–9.
8. **9 follow-ups on the VSL/booking phase** — After they've watched but haven't booked, follow up 9 more times. Same energy — memes, GIFs, funny, relentless but not desperate.
9. **Multi-channel expansion** — Find their email, phone, Facebook, LinkedIn. Follow up on ALL of them with the same sequence referencing the same CTA. Don't stay on one channel.
10. **The video does the closing, not you** — All follow-ups exist only to get them to watch the VSL. The VSL does the heavy lifting. Don't pitch on the follow-up — just get them to re-watch.
11. **10% appointment booking rate** — Achieved with this full system including all follow-ups. Most people give up after 3 follow-ups and get 0%.
