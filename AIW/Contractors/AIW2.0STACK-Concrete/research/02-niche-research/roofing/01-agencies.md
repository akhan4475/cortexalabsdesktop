## Top 3 Patterns Winners Share

1. **Roofing-exclusive positioning.** Hook Agency, King Contractor, RoofWebs, Profit Roofing all niche down to roofing only. Generalist agencies do not rank for these terms.
2. **Results-first language.** Winners sell leads and revenue, not pages and design. Losers sell features ("beautiful websites", "fast load times").
3. **Named trust signals.** GAF, Owens Corning, BBB, and Google Partner badges appear on nearly every winner's site.

## Top 3 Patterns Losers Share

1. **No pricing.** Every agency hides pricing. This is an opening -- a student who shows transparent pricing or a clear one-time offer can differentiate immediately.
2. **No AI or automation.** Zero agencies in this SERP mention AI chatbots, automated follow-up, or lead-to-CRM integration. The market has not caught up.
3. **Retainer-only model.** All agencies push ongoing retainers ($1,500-5,000/mo). No one serves the smaller roofer who wants a great one-time build with an AI chatbot for $600-1,200.
