## Top 3 Patterns Winners Share

1. **Niche or sub-niche exclusivity.** Evergrow (landscaping only), Lawnline (lawn care only), Landscape Leadership (landscape businesses only). Generalists don't rank for these queries.
2. **Outcomes language.** Winning agencies sell leads, revenue, and growth -- not design work. "All-Inclusive Marketing" (Lawnline), "Marketing & Sales for Lawn Care" (Landscape Leadership).
3. **Long content pages with specifics.** Winning agencies publish lists, how-tos, and niche case studies. Thin landing pages lose.

## Top 3 Patterns Losers Share

1. **No landscaping exclusivity.** Thrive and Straight North have "landscaping" pages but are clearly generalist. Prospects can tell.
2. **Template-only products.** LawnCareWebDesign offers no custom work and no AI. The product is commoditized and clients know it.
3. **No AI or chatbot differentiation.** Zero agencies in this SERP mention AI features. Same opening as roofing.
