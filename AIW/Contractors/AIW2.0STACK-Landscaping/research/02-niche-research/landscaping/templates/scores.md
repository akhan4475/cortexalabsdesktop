# Module 2D Phase 3: Site Scores
Niche: landscaping | Date: 2026-06-30
Rubric: 8 categories, 100 points total
(3 sites failed capture: scapesincorporated, outsidelandscapegroup, gibbslandscape)

---

## Scoring rubric

| Category | Points | What we look for |
|---|---|---|
| 1. CTA visibility above fold | 15 | Primary CTA clearly visible without scrolling, dominant accent color, click-to-call paired |
| 2. Trust signal density | 15 | Reviews, license number, badges, years in business visible early |
| 3. Hero clarity | 15 | Clear headline, aspirational imagery, portfolio visible |
| 4. Mobile pattern | 10 | Responsive nav, mobile sticky CTA, font legibility |
| 5. Visual coherence | 15 | Consistent palette, font pairing, premium feel, no clashing colors |
| 6. Navigation | 10 | Simple, niche-appropriate labels, no confusion |
| 7. Form / CTA friction | 10 | Low field count, clear value promise, warm label |
| 8. Conversion confidence | 10 | Would an anxious homeowner feel safe calling? |

## keanelandscaping.com -- 78/100

**1. CTA visibility (14/15):** Dual CTA above fold: "GET A QUOTE" button + "CALL: 972-424-4851" with phone icon, side by side. This is the best CTA mechanic in the pool. Loses 1pt because "Make Payment" button in the header nav creates visual competition.

**2. Trust signals (11/15):** "Rooted in Family. Built on Excellence. Trusted for 47 Years." Manufacturer partner logos (Texas Trees, etc.) in footer. No review count visible in the captured section. Loses 4pts for no review badge above fold.

**3. Hero clarity (9/15):** CTA section visible is clean and high-contrast but hero image section didn't render in the capture. Inferred from DOM: hero exists with headline. Loses 6pts for incomplete capture.

**4. Mobile (8/10):** Dual CTA pairing and nav structure with dropdown menus suggest mobile consideration. Footer newsletter form is low-friction.

**5. Visual coherence (10/15):** Dark green + gold/yellow color system. Works for landscaping but not distinctive -- the gold reads dated rather than premium. Font is clean sans-serif. Loses 5pts for generic visual language.

**6. Navigation (9/10):** Residential / Commercial segmentation in nav is smart -- qualifies the lead immediately. Portfolio + About + Contact are clear. Slight over-complexity with dropdown menus.

**7. Form friction (9/10):** "GET A QUOTE" + click-to-call pairing = minimal friction. Two clear paths for two types of buyers (form vs phone). Near-perfect.

**8. Conversion confidence (8/10):** 47-year tenure is a dominant trust anchor. Partner logos signal an established business. High confidence for a homeowner who just found them.

**Distinctive moves:** Dual CTA with call button is the strongest conversion pattern in the pool. 47-year tenure framing. Residential/Commercial nav split pre-qualifies leads.

**Anti-patterns:** Gold color ages the brand. No review count visible above fold. "Make Payment" in primary nav adds noise.
