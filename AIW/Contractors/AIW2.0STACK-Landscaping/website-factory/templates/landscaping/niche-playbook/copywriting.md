# Copywriting Contract: Landscaping Niche

This document defines the copy voice, formula, and section-level rules the copy-deck agent must follow when generating landscaping client copy. It supplements the universal copywriting skill with niche-specific parameters.

---

## Voice

- Register: calm and competent. Not excited, not salesy.
- Perspective: we/our (never "I" for multi-person crews).
- Tone words: direct, confident, professional, local.
- Sentence length: short. One idea per sentence.
- No em-dashes. No exclamation points. No buzzwords.

## Target reader

Homeowner, 35-60, $100k+ household income. Visual buyer. Anxious about reliability (will they show up? will it look good?). Spending $8-15k on a project. Needs confidence before committing.

## Banned words

leverage, synergize, seamless, game-changer, cutting-edge, robust, stunning, breathtaking, luxury, premier, bespoke, tailored solutions, take your outdoor living to the next level.

## Section rules

### Hero (H1)

Pattern: "Transform Your [City] Yard"
Variants allowed: "The [City] Landscaper Homeowners Trust" / "Your [City] Yard. Done Right."
Rule: city name in the H1 always. No metaphors. No adjective stacking.

### Hero subheadline

Pattern: "Responsive, on time, done right — from first call to final cleanup."
Rule: reference at least one reliability signal (on time, show up, responsive). Keep under 18 words.

### Services section

Label: "Our Services" (locked)
Each service card body: 1 sentence max. Describe the outcome, not the process.
Example: "We design and install full landscape transformations — from bare dirt to finished yard."

### Portfolio section

Label: "Our Work" (locked)
Caption format: [City, Service Type, Year]
No "stunning" or "beautiful" captions. Describe what was built, not how it looks.

### Why Choose Us

Each card: title = direct benefit claim, no body copy needed.
Formula: "[We do X] so that [you get Y without Z]"
Example: "We Show Up On Time" (implied: so you don't waste a day waiting)

### Process section

Section heading: "How It Works" (locked)
Steps: 3 max. Plain active verbs. No jargon.

### Reviews section

Heading formula: "[N] Happy Homeowners in [City]"
Pull verbatim from Google reviews — never paraphrase or clean up grammar.
Summary line: "[N]-star average across [N] Google reviews."

### Founder section

Tone: personal but professional. First name only in heading.
Para 1: how they started the business and why.
Para 2: what they are proud of / how they are different.
Vision/Mission: one sentence each. No corporate language.

### FAQ section

6 questions. Answers: 2-4 sentences each. Direct. No hedging.
Required topics: cost, free estimate, timing, insurance, irrigation (if offered), service area.

### CTA form

Header: "Get Your Free Landscape Estimate" (locked)
Subtext: "We respond within 2 hours. No commitment, no hard sell." (locked)
Submit button: "Request My Free Estimate" (locked)
Privacy line: "We never share your info. No spam. No hard sell." (locked)

## SEO copy rules

- H1 on homepage: "[City] Landscaping" or "Transform Your [City] Yard"
- Title tag pattern: "[Company] | [City] Landscaping Design & Installation"
- Meta description: 150-160 chars. Include "free estimate", city name, and one service.
- Service pages: one H1 per page targeting "[service] [city]"
- Location pages: one H1 per page targeting "Landscaping in [City]"
- FAQ: target "how much does landscaping cost [city]" and "best landscaper near me"
