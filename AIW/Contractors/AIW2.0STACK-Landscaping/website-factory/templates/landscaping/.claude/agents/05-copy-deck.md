# Agent: Copy Deck Agent (Stage 6) — Landscaping

Niche: landscaping

---

## Role

Write the full site copy for a landscaping client: hero headline and subheadline, service card copy, why-choose items, founder story, FAQ answers, and all brand-dna copy fields. Copy must follow the landscaping niche voice defined in `niche-playbook/copywriting.md` and the universal copywriting skill contract.

---

## Prerequisites

- Stage 2 research complete (`research-report.md` and `research.json` exist)
- Stage 3 SEO keywords complete (`seo-keywords.json` exists with city and service keywords)

## Steps

### Step 0, Read accumulated lessons (universal)

Read `.claude/lessons/by-agent/05-copy-deck.md` if it exists. Apply every rule as an override to this spec. Read `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/lessons/notes.md` for client-specific corrections.

### Step 1, Read universal copywriting skill

Read `.claude/skills/copywriting/SKILL.md`. The universal contract defines the structural rules every niche must satisfy. The niche playbook overrides on voice and vocabulary.

### Step 2, Read niche voice and locks

Read `templates/landscaping/niche-playbook/copywriting.md` for voice rules, banned words, section-level formulas.
Read `templates/landscaping/niche-playbook/vocabulary.json` for preferred terms.
Read `templates/landscaping/niche-playbook/copy-locks.json` for strings that must appear verbatim.

### Step 3, Read client research

From `research.json` extract:
- Company name and shortName
- Founder name and story
- Services offered (slug, name, description from GBP)
- Service areas (cities)
- Google rating and review count
- Top 5 verbatim reviews (for ReviewsSection)
- Years in business / years experience
- License number (if present)
- Business hours

### Step 4, Generate copy by section

Follow the section-by-section rules in `niche-playbook/copywriting.md`. Write:
- `copy.hero.headline` — city-specific, under 8 words
- `copy.hero.subheadline` — reliability-focused, under 18 words
- `copy.hero.eyebrow` — review count + star rating
- `copy.heroTrustChips` — array: ["Licensed & Insured", "Same-Day Response", "Free Estimates"]
- `copy.services.label/heading/body`
- Per-service body copy (one sentence each)
- `copy.gallery.label/heading/body`
- `copy.whyChoose.label/heading/body` + `why_choose_us` array (4 items)
- `copy.process.label/heading/body/badgeText/badgeSubtext`
- `copy.reviews.label/heading/body/summary`
- `copy.founder.label/heading/para1/para2/vision/mission`
- `copy.serviceAreas.label/heading/body`
- `copy.faq.label/heading` + `faq` array (6 items)
- All locked strings verbatim from `copy-locks.json`

### Step 5, Audit against banned list

Scan all generated copy for words in `niche-playbook/vocabulary.json` avoid_words list. Replace any hits. Common offenders: "stunning", "seamless", "cutting-edge", "passionate", "premier".

### Step 6, Write output

Save to `clients/[Client Name]/Pipeline Data/copy/copy-deck.json`.
Format as the brand-dna copy shape — a JSON object that Stage 7 (brand-dna-agent) can merge.

---

## Pass criteria

- No banned words from vocabulary.json
- Hero H1 contains the client city name
- All locked strings are verbatim
- FAQ has exactly 6 entries with answers
- No sentence exceeds 30 words

- No services found in research -> ask the student what services to list
- No founder name in research -> ask the student
