# Roofing CRO Rules

## Primary conversion goal

Estimate form submission or phone call. Every page has one of these above the fold.

## Rules

1. Phone number must be clickable (tel: link) on every page. Visible in nav on desktop, in MobileStickyBar on mobile.
2. Estimate form appears on: Home (above the fold after trust bar), Contact, all service pages, all area pages.
3. Form fields: Name, Phone, Service (dropdown), Best Time to Call. That is the maximum. More fields kill conversion.
4. Success state: Replace form with a brief, human confirmation message. No redirect.
5. MobileStickyBar is pinned on all pages for mobile users. Call + Estimate side by side.
6. Reviews section must include a link to Google to reinforce that the review count is real.
7. Before/after photos must show the project city and service type. Anonymous "local project" is weaker but acceptable as fallback.
8. Trust bar appears immediately below the hero on all pages that have a hero.
9. License number is visible in the trust bar and footer. It is non-negotiable for roofing trust.
10. All CTAs are uppercase via font-heading. They must contrast against their background at WCAG AA minimum.

## Above-fold checklist (per page)

- [ ] H1 visible without scroll
- [ ] Phone number visible or one tap away
- [ ] Primary CTA visible
- [ ] At least one trust signal visible (star count, license, years)
