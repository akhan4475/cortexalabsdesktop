## Hero headline pattern

Pull from one of these patterns (city-anchor required):

1. `YOUR [CITY] ROOF. DONE ONCE, DONE RIGHT.` -- fear reversal
2. `[N]+ [CITY] HOMEOWNERS CHOSE US. HERE'S WHY.` -- social proof + local
3. `FREE ROOF INSPECTION -- NO PRESSURE, NO SALES GAMES.` -- objection removal
4. `STORM DAMAGE? WE HANDLE THE WHOLE PROCESS.` -- trigger-event hook
5. `[CITY]'S GAF CERTIFIED ROOFER. FREE ESTIMATE TODAY.` -- cert + local

H1 must be uppercase. Max 2 lines at desktop size.

## Primary CTA

`Get a Free Estimate` or `Get My Free Estimate`. Never: "Contact Us", "Get Started", "Learn More", "Submit".

## Secondary CTA

`Call [PHONE]` -- always includes the formatted phone number on desktop. Mobile uses tel-link.

## End-customer phrases to echo (top 5)

These or their direct equivalents must appear somewhere in the copy:

1. "no pressure, no unnecessary upselling"
2. "communication really set them apart"
3. "cleanup every day" / "left it cleaner than they found it"
4. "done in one day"
5. "worked with our insurance adjuster"

## End-customer fears to address in FAQ (minimum 5)

1. How long does a roof replacement take?
2. Do you work with insurance? What is the process?
3. Are you licensed and insured? (Show the actual license number)
4. What areas do you serve?
5. How much does a roof replacement cost? (Give a range)

## Banned phrases (roofing additions to universal blocklist)

industry-leading, top-rated, premium quality, world-class, state-of-the-art, revolutionary, disruptive, passion, passionate, seamless, solutions

## Trust elements -- lead in this order

1. Google review count + star rating (number must appear)
2. GAF/OC certification OR license number
3. License number (actual number, not just "licensed & insured")
4. Years in business
5. Before/after gallery (real jobs, real locations, not stock)

## Section order (HomePage) -- do not reorder

Hero, TrustBar, EstimateForm, ServicesGrid, WhyUs, BeforeAfterGallery, TeamSection, ReviewTiles, ProcessSteps, InsuranceSection, FAQAccordion, FinalCTA, Footer
