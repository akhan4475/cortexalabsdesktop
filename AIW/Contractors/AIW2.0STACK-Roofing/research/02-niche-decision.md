CHOSEN NICHE: roofing

Why this one over the others:
- One closed job from a website pays for the build. At $8-12k average ticket, the ROI conversation is immediate and obvious. A roofer does not need to see 10 leads to feel the value.
- Ayaan's uncle owns a construction and roofing business. That gives direct access to niche language, client pain points, and a potential first case study without cold outreach.
- The market has no AI automation yet. No agency in the roofing SERP offers a chatbot, automated follow-up, or CRM integration. The Voiceflow + Make.com stack is a genuine differentiator that no competitor at any price point is currently selling.

Positioning locked: low-ticket one-time build ($1,000-2,000) with AI chatbot included. Targets smaller roofers (20-100 reviews, $500k-2M/year) who cannot afford Hook Agency or King Contractor retainers and are currently getting nothing from their online presence.

Niche customer reality check:
- They research before buying: yes -- homeowners compare 2-3 roofers, read reviews, and check websites before calling
- Average ticket: $8,000-12,000 per residential job
- They hang out at: Google Maps, Nextdoor, Facebook neighborhood groups, insurance adjuster referrals
- Website ROI math: 1 job closed from a website lead = $9,000 average. A $1,200 website pays for itself in the first week it generates a single estimate request. At 1 close per 5 leads, a site generating 10 leads/month = $18,000/month in new revenue for the client.

5 example businesses in this niche Ayaan could approach this week:
- Uncle's roofing/construction business (direct contact, warm relationship) -- first case study candidate, no cold outreach needed
- Any roofing company in the Baltimore/DMV area with 20-80 Google reviews, no website or a dated one -- search "roofing near me" + filter by review count, check their web presence
- Storm-damage-focused roofer in a Sun Belt market (Dallas, Atlanta, Houston) -- higher urgency, year-round demand, less seasonal risk; find via Google Maps + "storm damage roofing [city]"
- A roofer actively running Facebook or Nextdoor ads but with a weak landing page -- the ad spend signals willingness to pay for leads; find via Facebook Ad Library search "roofing [city]"
- A roofer with 50+ reviews but no website showing on their GMB profile -- they are already winning on trust signals but leaking leads; high-intent prospect for a fast close
