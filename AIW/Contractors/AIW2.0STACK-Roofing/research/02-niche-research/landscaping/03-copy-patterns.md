## Common Ad Hook Structures [inferred]

1. **Aspiration trigger:** "Imagine stepping into your backyard and loving what you see. Here's how we make that happen."
2. **Season hook:** "Spring is here. Is your yard ready? [Free spring cleanup estimate]"
3. **Neighbor comparison:** "[City] homeowners are redesigning their backyards. Here's what's trending."
4. **Fear reversal:** "Tired of landscapers who don't show up? We're different. Here's proof."
5. **Before/after hook:** Side-by-side image of a yard transformation, no text needed.
