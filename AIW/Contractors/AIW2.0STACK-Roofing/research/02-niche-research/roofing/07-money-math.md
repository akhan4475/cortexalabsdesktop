# 07 - Money Math: Roofing

Source: `raw/maps-reviews.json` (transaction signals), `01-agencies.md` (agency case study references), `raw/serp-rankings.json` ("roof repair cost" SERP data).

---

## Average Ticket Size

- **Roof repair:** $400-$1,500 (source: SERP "roof repair cost" results citing national averages of $1,150 avg)
- **Full roof replacement:** $6,000-$12,000 for residential (source: SERP "roof replacement cost" result citing $6k-$9k average; upper range from review mentions of $8-12k jobs)
- **Insurance claim jobs:** Often $8,000-$15,000+ (storm damage includes full replacement + materials upgrade)
- **Emergency tarp + board-up:** $300-$800 (gateway service that leads to full replacement contract)

**Working number: $8,000-$10,000 average replacement job. $1,200 average repair.**

---

## Estimated Close Rate per Qualified Lead

- Industry estimate for residential roofing: 20-35% of qualified inspections convert to a paid job. [inferred from agency case study language + review volume to job frequency signals]
- A "qualified lead" = homeowner who requests an estimate, has a real roof issue, and owns the property.
- Post-storm surge: close rate can exceed 40% if response time is fast (same-day visit wins over competitors who take 3-5 days to respond).

**Working number: 25% close rate on qualified leads from website.**

---

## Leads Needed Per Month

To generate $10,000/month in additional revenue for the client:
- At $9,000 average ticket x 25% close rate = need 4.4 qualified leads per month
- Round up: **5 qualified leads/month from the website converts to ~1 new job/month = $9,000 revenue**

For the student's pitch math:
- 1 new job/month from website leads = $9,000 revenue
- Website cost: $600-1,200 one-time
- Ongoing maintenance/retainer: $150-300/month
- **ROI: Website pays for itself with the first job. Every subsequent job is pure upside.**

---

## The Pitch Sentence

"If your website gets you just 5 qualified estimate requests a month -- which is realistic based on what we're seeing for similar roofers in [City] -- and you close 1 out of 5, that's one extra $9,000 job per month. Your website pays for itself in the first week it generates a lead."

## Supporting Evidence from Raw Data

- Homeowners explicitly mention getting 3+ quotes (reviews) -- the website must win the pre-call impression to be one of those 3.
- SERP for "roof replacement near me" shows directories dominating -- a well-optimized GMB listing + website combo is how a local roofer breaks into the Maps pack and captures this traffic.
- Storm events drive emergency search surges (multiple review mentions of post-storm jobs) -- a site that ranks locally captures this burst demand.
