Gravity FAQ’s

How can I tell if my roof needs repairs or a replacement?

Signs that your roof may need attention include missing, cracked, or curling shingles, leaks inside your home, or visible sagging. If you notice any of these issues, contact us for a free inspection. We’ll assess the condition of your roof and recommend either repairs or a replacement based on the severity of the damage, the age of your roof, and overall structural condition.

What should I do if my roof is damaged in a storm?

First, ensure your safety and avoid going onto the roof. Document the damage with photos from the ground if possible. Cover the damaged area with a tarp if it’s safe to do so to prevent further damage. Then, contact your insurance company to report the damage, and call Gravity Roofing for a professional assessment and to discuss repair options.

How does Gravity Roofing handle roofing insurance claims?

We simplify the insurance claims process by conducting a thorough assessment, documenting the damage, and directly communicating with your insurance provider to facilitate your claim. Our team acts as your advocate to ensure you receive fair compensation. We handle all the paperwork and necessary communications, making the process as stress-free as possible for you.

Are there preventative measures I can take to protect my roof?

Yes, regular inspections and maintenance are key to prolonging the life of your roof. We recommend scheduling routine inspections with us, especially after severe weather events. We also offer preventative solutions like protective coatings that enhance your roof’s resistance to weather and aging.

What makes Gravity Roofing different from other roofing contractors?

At Gravity Roofing, we pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, safety, and ethical practices. We are fully licensed and insured, and we use only the highest quality materials suited for Florida’s unique climate conditions. Our team is composed of experienced professionals passionate about delivering exceptional workmanship and customer service.

How do I choose the right roofing material for my property?

The choice of roofing material depends on several factors, including your aesthetic preference, budget, and the structural needs of your building. We offer a variety of roofing materials, including shingles, tile, metal, and flat roofing options. During your free consultation, we can discuss the benefits of each material and help you make an informed decision based on your specific needs.

What are the risks of hiring an unlicensed contractor?

Hiring an unlicensed contractor can expose you to numerous risks, such as subpar work, no warranty on the work performed, potential legal and financial liabilities if an injury occurs on your property, and the possibility of fraud. Always verify a contractor’s license and insurance to ensure they are qualified to perform the work legally and safely.

How long does a typical roof installation take?

The duration of a roof installation can vary based on the size of the roof, the type of materials being used, and weather conditions. For most residential properties, a new roof can be installed within 1-3 days. Larger or more complex projects, such as commercial buildings or roofs requiring special materials, may take longer. We strive to minimize disruption and complete the work efficiently, ensuring high-quality results.
