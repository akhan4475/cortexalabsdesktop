    Does Lyons Roofing install roofing before or after solar panel installation?

    Lyons Roofing recommends installing or replacing the roof before solar panels are installed if the roof is approaching end of life. Solar systems are typically warranted for 20 to 25 years, and if a roof replacement is needed during that period the panels must be removed and reinstalled at additional cost. Installing a new roof first avoids this expense.

    What is the best roofing material for an Arizona home?

    Clay and concrete tile is the most durable long-term choice in Arizona’s desert climate due to its non-combustibility, UV resistance, and proven local performance record. Architectural shingles provide good performance at lower upfront cost. Spray foam offers the highest insulating value for flat sections. The right choice depends on the home’s structure, HOA requirements, budget, and the homeowner’s maintenance preferences. Lyons Roofing presents all options honestly during consultations.

    What should I do when my roof starts leaking?

    If your roof is actively leaking, contain interior water with buckets and move valuables away from the leak area. Do not attempt to access the roof during rain or lightning. Call Lyons Roofing at 602-755-1529 in Phoenix or 520-582-9455 in Tucson immediately. Our emergency crews are available 24 hours a day to deploy tarping and temporary protection as soon as safe access is available.
