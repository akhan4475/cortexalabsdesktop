FAQ’s

Questions? We’re Glad that you asked!

How do I know if my roof needs to be repaired or completely replaced?

It depends on the age of your roof and the extent of the damage. If you’re dealing with ongoing leaks, widespread wear, or a roof that’s over 15–20 years old, replacement may be more cost-effective. A professional inspection can help you decide the right path.

Why do roofs in Arizona wear out faster than in other places?

Arizona’s harsh climate, scorching sun, dry air, and heavy monsoon rains—can break down roofing materials much quicker than in cooler regions. Shingles can crack, tiles can loosen, and flat roofs can develop ponding issues. Using materials suited to this climate is essential for longevity.

What should I look for when hiring a roofing contractor?

Look for a company that is licensed, insured, and has solid local experience. Check reviews, ask for references, and make sure they offer written estimates and warranties. Avoid anyone who pressures you or asks for full payment upfront.

How much does roof repair usually cost?

Costs can vary depending on the type of damage, the material of your roof, and the size of the repair. Minor repairs may be a few hundred dollars, while larger structural repairs can be in the thousands. A free inspection is the best way to get an accurate quote.

Can I still get my roof fixed during monsoon season?

Yes, but timing is important. During the rainy season, even small leaks can quickly turn into major problems. Many roofing companies offer emergency services or temporary protection to keep your home safe until permanent repairs can be made.

How often should my roof be checked or maintained?

At least once a year is recommended, especially before the rainy season. Regular maintenance can catch small issues—like cracked tiles or lifted flashing—before they turn into expensive repairs. It also helps extend the life of your roof.

What kind of warranties should I expect with a new roof?

Warranties vary by material and installer, but many roofing systems come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 10 to 50 years. Reputable contractors also offer a workmanship guarantee, covering the quality of their labor for added peace of mind.
