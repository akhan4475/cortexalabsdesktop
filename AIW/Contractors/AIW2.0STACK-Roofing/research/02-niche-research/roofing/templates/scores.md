### Distinctive Moves
- Roof Care Club: exclusive maintenance membership -- recurring revenue built in
- County + city location page hierarchy (county index -> city pages)

- Free consultation framing (not "free quote") -- lower anxiety

### Anti-Patterns
- Dog theme buries immediate value prop on first scan
- No financing or 24/7 emergency in trust bar
- Hero is image-heavy, minimal scannable text
