## Top 3 Patterns Winners Share

1. **Sub-niche separation.** Pool Marketing targets pool companies, Brand Constructors targets pool builders, Prime Media targets gutter cleaning. Niche-within-niche wins. "Home services websites" loses to "pool service websites."
2. **Integration pitch.** ServiceAutopilot bundles scheduling software with the website. The trend is toward platforms (website + booking + CRM in one). This is an opening for AI chatbot + automation to fill the same need.
3. **Visual proof heavy.** Pool websites are visual. Every winning agency shows portfolio images prominently. The before/after format works here too (clean pool vs. green pool, clear gutters vs. clogged gutters).

## Top 3 Patterns Losers Share

1. **Generic digital agency with a niche page.** Grown Nearby and Cyberoptik are not pool/gutter exclusive. Prospects can tell and don't trust it.
2. **Ads-only focus.** Prime Media sells Google Ads, not websites. These are different budget lines for the client and create a gap for a website-first product.
3. **No AI or automation.** Zero agencies mention chatbots, automated quote requests, or CRM integration. Same gap as roofing and landscaping.
