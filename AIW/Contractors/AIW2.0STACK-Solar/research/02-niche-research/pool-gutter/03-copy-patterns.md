## Common Ad Hook Structures [inferred]

1. **Season trigger:** "Storm season is coming. Are your gutters ready? Book now." (Fall/winter in most markets)
2. **Reliability contrast:** "Tired of pool guys who don't show up? We've served [N] [City] pools this year."
3. **Photo-proof hook:** Before/after image of clean gutters vs. blocked, debris-full gutters. No text needed.
4. **Price anchor:** "Gutter cleaning from $X. No hidden fees. Book in 60 seconds."
5. **Peace of mind:** "Every visit documented. Every job guaranteed. Book now."
