4. Verify "No contracts" claim appears in at least 2 separate DOM locations. Check trust bar and form privacy line.
5. Verify trust bar renders on all 4 pillar service pages (pool-cleaning, maintenance, repair, opening-closing).
6. Verify FAQ has minimum 5 items. If brand-dna.faq is empty, confirm fallback FAQ in component renders correctly.
7. Verify before/after photos are present in Gallery, OR `MANUAL-DROP-NEEDED.md` is logged in assets.
8. Verify phone number is a `tel:` link in nav and in footer (not just text).
9. Log all issues found. Fix or flag as MANUAL items.

## Pass criteria

- All 9 uplift checks above pass or are documented as MANUAL.
- No automatic HALT conditions (see below).

## When this stage halts

- `heroTrustChips` is empty AND cannot be auto-populated from trust-signals: halt, request student to provide at least 3 trust chip strings.
- Pricing section is completely absent from all pages: halt, add Pricing component to homepage in build.

## Niche-specific notes

Pool service CRO rules are documented in `niche-playbook/cro-rules.md`. The three most impactful rules for this niche:
1. Pricing visibility (pool buyers filter on price harder than most niches)
2. "No contracts" claim in multiple places (major objection in this niche)
3. Before/after photography (visual proof converts more than copy in pool services)
