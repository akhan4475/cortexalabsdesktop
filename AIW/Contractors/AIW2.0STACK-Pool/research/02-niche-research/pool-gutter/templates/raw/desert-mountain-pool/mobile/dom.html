Welcome and thank you for considering Desert Mountain Pool Care & Landscape Design for your outdoor needs.

We are a full-service pool and spa company servicing the Greater Desert Mountain area, including Cave Creek and surrounding communities.  We offer monthly pool, spa, hot-tub maintenance, as well as repairs and equipment replacement.  We know your pool and spa is your oasis and we pride ourselves in providing exceptional, timely service you can rely upon.

There are many pool companies, and it can be difficult to find one with consistent, high-quality service.  With our company, we are proud of being Cave Creek’s go-to source for all their pool and spa needs.  We are a family-owned business, we understand having a personal touch and we will go above and beyond to meet your specific needs.

We are committed to providing the best service to our customers.  If you need a new pool service company or would like a second opinion, we will be more than happy to inspect your pool and equipment giving you the peace of mind that comes with knowing your pool is in good hands.
