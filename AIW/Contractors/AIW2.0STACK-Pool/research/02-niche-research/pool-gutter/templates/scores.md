## 1. Patriot Pool & Spa -- patriotpoolandspa.com (DFW, TX) -- 78/100

| Category | Score | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| CTA visibility | 13/15 | "Schedule Service" appears 6+ times across page sections. Phone 8+ times. Highly aggressive and effective CTA density. |
| Trust signals | 11/15 | 4.8 Google stars, 30+ years, Pool Vision training, NPP member, 6 named Google testimonials. Lacks visible certification badge strip. |
| Hero clarity | 11/15 | "Premium Pool Maintenance / Experience the Patriot Difference" -- direct service label, 30 year claim support. Subline is generic. |
| Mobile pattern | 8/10 | Icon-heavy UI (Font Awesome 7), navy/cyan contrast holds well on small screens. |
| Visual coherence | 11/15 | Navy (#164278) + cyan (#27C3F4) + red (#D63230) -- professional, clear hierarchy, consistent across sections. |
| Navigation | 9/10 | Best navigation of all 10: weekly, services, repair, locations (11 areas), about. Logical hierarchy. |
| Form friction | 6/10 | "Schedule online or give us a call" exists but no on-page form. CTA goes to separate page. |
| Conversion confidence | 9/10 | Strong package: reviews, years, service breadth, location coverage, aggressive CTAs. |

**Distinctive moves:**
- CTA repetition pattern (x6 Schedule Service) -- no other site does this
- Cyan (#27C3F4) as accent against dark navy -- readable and distinctive
- 11-location navigation structure (scalable model for area-based service pages)

**Anti-patterns:**
- "Experience the Patriot Difference" is meaningless as a subline
- No on-page lead capture form
- Chatbot cited in external reviews as a differentiator but not visible in current site

## 2. Payan Pool Service -- payanpools.com (San Diego, CA) -- 76/100

| Category | Score | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| CTA visibility | 12/15 | "Get Free Quote" in header, "Get In Touch" hero, "Schedule Service" bottom CTA strip. Phone x4. Good spread but no booking widget in hero. |
| Trust signals | 13/15 | 35+ years, BBB Accredited, IPSSA certified, CPO certified, 6 named testimonials, structured schema markup (aggregateRating 5.0/18 reviews). Best trust stack of all 10. |
| Hero clarity | 11/15 | "Caring For Your California Dream Since 1987" -- heritage-led, warm, but not a service-first headline. Works for repeat visitors. |
| Mobile pattern | 7/10 | Inter font + responsive Tailwind layout. Clean on mobile but no sticky booking bar. |
| Visual coherence | 12/15 | Deep blue/cyan gradient (#0D6CB6 + #2EA3F2) with orange accent (#F58026). Inter typography. Modern and coherent. Best color system of all 10. |

**Distinctive moves:**
- Full schema markup (LocalBusiness + AggregateRating + service hours) -- best SEO foundation

- Certification badge strip (BBB + IPSSA + CPO) -- most credible trust signal combination

**Anti-patterns:**
- "California Dream" positioning is regional, not transferable
- No online booking or scheduling widget
- Contact form requires navigation away from homepage
