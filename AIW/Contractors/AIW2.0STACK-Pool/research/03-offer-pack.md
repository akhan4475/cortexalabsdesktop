## Customer Voice: Phrases to Echo

Verbatim from real homeowner reviews in this niche. Use this language - it mirrors what their customers are already thinking.

1. "hit or miss" - the industry reputation. Name the problem: "Most pool service sites look hit or miss too."
2. "before and after pictures = peace of mind" - the single strongest conversion feature in this niche.
3. "no hidden fees" / "fair and transparent pricing" - a pricing section earns trust before the call happens.
4. "reliable" / "reliability" - the brand promise the whole site is built around.
5. "professional crew" - the fear is an unskilled random contractor. The site combats this.
6. "flexible with timing" - scheduling ease. Online booking removes this friction.
7. "will continue using" / "for years" - long-term retention is the business model.
8. "solid communications" - the one thing separating top reviews from 1-star reviews.

---

## Top 3 Trust Signals to Lead With

1. Before/after photo documentation. "Before and after pictures = peace of mind" is verbatim from a 5-star review. Every site you build has a before/after section. Non-negotiable.

2. Google review count and star rating in the hero. The niche has an industry-wide "hit or miss" reputation. Reviews above the fold immediately separate this company from that reputation.

3. Transparent pricing section. "No hidden fees" praised specifically in multiple reviews. Showing even a starting-rate range converts homeowners who fear a surprise invoice.

## DM Structure: Pain / Offer / CTA

- Pain: open with something specific about their situation that is costing them jobs. Not generic.
- Offer: sell the desired outcome - new booked clients, recurring revenue - not the tools or features.
- CTA: low ask. Send a video, not a call. Goal of message 1 is message 2.
- Tone: blue-collar, chill, typed on a phone. Not corporate. Not perfectly written.
- Mockup: always say you built one. Build it only for the ones who say yes - filters the no's fast.
- No double dashes ever. Single dash or comma only.
