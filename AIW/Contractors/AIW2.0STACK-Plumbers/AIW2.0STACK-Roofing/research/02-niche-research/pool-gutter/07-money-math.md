# 07 - Money Math: Pool & Gutter

Source: `raw/serp-rankings.json` ("gutter cleaning cost" SERP), `raw/maps-reviews.json` (transaction signals), `01-agencies.md`.

---

## Average Ticket Size

**Gutter Cleaning:**
- Single-story home: $100-150 per cleaning (source: SERP "gutter cleaning cost" results citing $119-234 average, $168 national median)
- Two-story home: $150-300 per cleaning
- Annual cleaning frequency: 2x per year typical, 4x in heavy-tree markets
- Annual client LTV: $200-600 per home

**Pool Cleaning:**
- Weekly pool service: $150-400/month depending on market and pool size
- Monthly service (chemical only): $75-150/month
- Pool opening/closing: $150-350 per event
- Pool repair: $200-1,500 per repair depending on issue
- Pool resurfacing: $3,000-10,000
- Annual recurring LTV (weekly service): $1,800-4,800/year per home

**Pool Installation:**
- Inground pool: $30,000-80,000+ (high-ticket, not Ayaan's target segment)

**Working numbers for pitch math:**
- Gutter client: $300-500/year LTV (2-3 cleanings)
- Pool cleaning client: $2,400-4,800/year LTV (weekly or biweekly service)

---

## Estimated Close Rate per Qualified Lead

- Gutter cleaning: 30-45% (low-consideration, price-driven) [inferred]
- Pool service: 25-35% (slightly more considered -- homeowners compare options)
- **Working number: 35% close rate on qualified leads from website**

---

## Leads Needed Per Month

**Gutter cleaning:**
- At $300/year LTV x 35% close rate: need 3 qualified leads to get 1 customer ($300/year)
- To generate $3,000/year in new recurring revenue: need ~10 new clients = 29 leads per month (high volume needed)
- Better framing: 5 new gutter clients per month from the website = $1,500/year in new recurring revenue = $7,500 over 5 years per customer if they stay

**Pool cleaning:**
- At $3,600/year LTV x 35% close rate: need 3 qualified leads to get 1 client ($3,600/year)
- 2 new pool clients per month from the website = $7,200/year in new recurring revenue
- **ROI: Website pays for itself in 2-3 months of new pool cleaning clients**

---

## The Pitch Sentence (Pool)

"If your website books just 2 new weekly pool service clients a month, that's $7,200 in new recurring revenue per year. Those clients typically stay 3-5 years, so your $800 website generates $21,000-36,000 in lifetime value from clients it found for you."

## The Pitch Sentence (Gutter)

"Most homeowners need gutters cleaned 2-3 times a year. If your website generates 5 new clients a month, that's $750-1,500 in new monthly bookings. Your website pays for itself in the first month of new jobs."

---

## Key Note for the Student

Pool cleaning in Sun Belt markets (Phoenix especially) is the stronger pitch. Year-round revenue, $3,600+/year per client LTV, and website ROI is clear and fast. Gutter cleaning is seasonal and lower-ticket -- still worth building for, but the ROI story is weaker and the client needs volume.

If the student picks pool-gutter as a niche, push clients toward pool-dominant messaging (or position as "pool + gutter" to hit both service lines).
